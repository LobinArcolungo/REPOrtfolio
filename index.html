Get to know me!

Hey! It's Giacomo Cerino and I'm a Software Developer located in Copenhagen. I've worked with startups, abroad companies and consulted for Italian professionals and companies

I worked with Python, C++ ,Docker, Ros2 in Robotics environments. But also with Rhino3d, Tensorflow, Java, Bash during my internships and university projects. My studies as a computer engineer on every aspect of the computer functioning: from the bare metal architecture of caches, through cpu pipelines, a bit of assembly, to operative systems, programming languages, multi-process and multi-machine computation and through network, artificial intelligence, web and cloud technologies. Still, what I grew most passionate about is fast prototyping, Robotics, IoT.
I love finding solutions and helping people with smart ideas, so don't hesitate to contact me here!
