The Window full of honey

A good friend that helped us a lot with setting up the beeboxes one day called us saying that inside the house that he just bought, he found a hive inside a window. We went checking of course and we found a family of bees that despite being there since few weeks (you can see it from how white the hive is), had already built a huge beehive. With our surprise, we noticed that because of the amount of flowering ivy in the surroundings, it was for a good part full of honey. And so we managed to get some of what we called "Window honey".

Acacia honey
